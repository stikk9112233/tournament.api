# 🎮 Tournament API - Free Fire Tournament Platform

A comprehensive tournament management system for Free Fire esports tournaments with real-time leaderboards, prize distribution, and match management.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏆 Tournament Management
- ✅ Create tournaments with custom entry fees
- ✅ Set prize pools and distribution across game modes
- ✅ Support for 3 modes: Bermuda, Clash Squad, Lone Wolf
- ✅ Automatic 10% platform commission calculation
- ✅ Manage multiple matches per tournament

### 🎯 Match Management
- ✅ Schedule matches with specific times
- ✅ Generate Free Fire room IDs and passwords
- ✅ Track player kills, positions, and booyahs
- ✅ Real-time score entry system

### 📊 Leaderboard & History
- ✅ Personal player leaderboards per tournament
- ✅ Complete playing history across all tournaments
- ✅ Track stats: kills, booyahs, earnings
- ✅ Prize breakdown by match

### 💰 Prize Distribution
- ✅ Booyah prize (for 1st position)
- ✅ Per-kill prize (₹5-7 per kill)
- ✅ Automatic calculation and display
- ✅ Hidden commission from players (10%)
